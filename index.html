VDSR Policy

VDSR app uses the camera rights to scan the barcode and decodes it locally on the device. The information stored in the barcode is only processed on the device, but not stored.

VDSR retrieves current certificates and CRLs for verification of the signature contained in the barcode from our personal GitHub.

VDSR will not use or collect your personal data for any purpose other than that stated here. There is no advertising and no tracking.
